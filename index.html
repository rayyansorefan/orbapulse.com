Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What data we collect

Orba Pulse stores your mood entries, reflections, and app preferences locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.

Analytics

We use anonymous, aggregated analytics to understand how the app is used (e.g., which features are most popular). This data cannot identify you personally.

Third-party services

If you subscribe to Orba Pulse Premium, your subscription is managed through Apple's App Store or Google Play. We use RevenueCat to process subscription data. We do not have access to your payment details.

Data you create

  • Mood entries and reflections are stored on your device only
  • You can export or delete your data at any time from the app settings
  • We never sell, share, or monetise your personal data

Your rights

You can delete all your data at any time by using the "Delete All Data" option in the app settings, or by uninstalling the app. If you have any questions, contact us at hello@orbapulse.com.

Children's privacy

Orba Pulse is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
